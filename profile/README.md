### Quantum Approaches to Protein Folding

Welcome to the **QFold-Thesis** organization. This repository collection constitutes the software artifacts and documentation for our **Bachelor of Engineering (B.Eng.) Thesis**.

Our work focuses on the practical implementation, analysis, and extension of quantum algorithms for the **Protein Folding Problem**, building upon the foundational frameworks provided by Qiskit and recent academic publications.
